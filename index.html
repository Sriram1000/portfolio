About Me

I'm a junior at Emerald High School in Dublin, California, with a deep passion for computer science and artificial intelligence. I plan to major in CS and pursue a career at a top technology company, driven by a belief that software and AI have the potential to solve some of the world's most complex problems. I'm actively seeking summer 2026 AI internships in Research, Data Science, or Software Development.

One of the things I'm most proud of is founding and leading the EHS AI Club, which has grown to 60+ members across 50+ meetups. I designed and delivered a multi-semester curriculum covering Traditional ML, Deep Learning, and GenAI/LLMs, and launched bi-weekly outreach sessions at Fallon Middle School to extend AI literacy to younger students.

I'm currently a Student Assistant at TipplyAI, a climate science AI startup founded by a MILA researcher, where I work on atmospheric causal modeling and help identify AI-enabled tools to tackle emerging climate threats.

In the summer of 2025, I completed an intensive Data Science Lab at UC Berkeley's ATDP program, earning an A in a college-level course covering exploratory data analysis, probability, significance testing, and machine learning, all using R. The FIFA 21 data dashboard I built there is one of the projects I'm most proud of.

Outside of academics and coding, I'm a Varsity Soccer Goalkeeper with 11+ years at competitive clubs across Dublin, San Ramon, and Oakland. I also serve as a Teaching Assistant at California Tamil Academy, where I help students navigate Tamil language instruction after completing 10 years of formal Tamil education. I care about giving back to my community and staying connected to my heritage.
